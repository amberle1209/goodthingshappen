## Problem Statement

Gratitude journaling has proven mental health benefits, but every existing app fails at the same thing: retention. Users download, write "grateful for family" three days in a row, get bored, and delete. The $310M gratitude app market has 43-66% dropout rates because the core loop offers no emotional payoff after writing.

The founder wants to build a product that flips this: instead of "write and forget," the product turns 3 daily good things into a visually stunning, shareable calendar card. The recording is the input; the AI-generated scene is the reward.

## Demand Evidence

No direct demand evidence yet. This is a pure-intuition start. However:
- The gratitude app market is $310M (2024) growing at 15.2% CAGR, proving sustained demand
- Dropout rates of 43-66% prove people WANT to do this but existing products fail them
- No app has cracked viral sharing of gratitude content yet, which is the gap this product targets
- AI-generated visual art from journal entries is the #1 trend in the space for 2026 (text + image = 6x memory retention vs text alone)

**Validation needed:** 20 strangers use the MVP; at least 3 share their card on social media without being asked.

## Status Quo

Current user workarounds:
1. Text-based gratitude apps (Gratitude, Day One, Calm) - boring after 3 days, no shareability
2. Paper journals - private, no social element, easy to abandon
3. Nothing - most people who "want to be more grateful" do nothing because the friction is too high and the payoff is too low

The real competitor is "doing nothing." The status quo wins because existing solutions don't produce anything worth showing anyone.

## Target User & Narrowest Wedge

**Target:** English-speaking wellness-curious adults (25-40) who use Instagram/X/TikTok. People who post about self-improvement, mindfulness, manifestation, or "that girl" aesthetic content.

**Narrowest wedge:** A PWA where you type 3 good things and get a beautiful AI-generated calendar card to share. No social features. No community. No subscription (initially). Just: input 3 things, get art, share it.

**Why this user:** They already post lifestyle content. The product gives them a new daily content format that's personal, positive, and visually distinctive. The "Law of Attraction" framing taps into the manifestation community, which is large and active on social media.

## Constraints

- Founder based in China, target market is US/global English speakers
- 10-15 hours/week side business budget
- No existing codebase, user base, or domain expertise in wellness/mental health
- Must validate sharing behavior before investing in retention features
- AI image generation cost target: $0.05/card. If API costs exceed this, must either switch to a cheaper API or implement caching/template strategies to reduce per-card cost

## Premises

1. The product must have 10x differentiation in at least one dimension (visual output quality + shareability), though it doesn't need to create an entirely new category
2. User retention's root cause is "no reward after writing." Each recording must produce a visually compelling, shareable output
3. Target market is English-speaking users (US priority), distributed via web (PWA) and social sharing
4. Sharing is the growth engine, not an add-on feature. If no one shares, the product fails
5. The calendar card format (date header + AI scene + 3 good things) is the core shareable artifact

## Approaches Considered

### Approach A: "Daily Good Things Card" (Minimal Viable)
PWA. User inputs 3 good things, AI generates a beautiful shareable card (static image), one-tap share to Instagram/X. Each card has a unique style based on content.
- Effort: S (48 hours to prototype)
- Risk: Low
- Pros: Fastest to validate core sharing hypothesis; cost ~$0.05/card
- Cons: No retention mechanism beyond streaks; no social elements
- Reuses: Standard Next.js + Vercel stack

### Approach B: "Good Things Garden" (Ideal Architecture)
PWA + social. Daily recording generates a growing "garden" - each good thing becomes a flower. Users see others' gardens. Weekly/monthly Spotify Wrapped-style reviews.
- Effort: M-L (2-4 weeks)
- Risk: Medium
- Pros: Strong retention via visual growth; natural sharing material
- Cons: Garden metaphor may disconnect from content; heavy build for unvalidated idea

### Approach C: "Unboxing" Mode (Creative/Lateral)
Reversed design. Product gives you a daily "prediction" card each morning; you return at night to record whether it came true and what actually happened. Leverages "Law of Attraction" mystery.
- Effort: M (1-2 weeks)
- Risk: High (positioning as fortune-telling vs wellness)
- Pros: Strong curiosity/sharing hook ("my prediction came true!")
- Cons: May alienate wellness audience; scientific credibility issues

## Recommended Approach

**Approach A with B's visual language** (the "minimum slice" strategy the founder chose).

Build the MVP as a daily calendar card generator:
- Input: 3 text fields for good things
- Processing: GPT-4o mini synthesizes a scene prompt from the 3 inputs, image generation API creates the visual
- Output: A calendar-style card with date header, AI-generated scene (landscape/map style with 3 markers), the 3 good things as text, and Bloom branding
- Share: Download card image + open share target (see Sharing Constraints below)

The calendar card is the product. Every card has:
1. A tear-off calendar style date header (black bar with white date)
2. An AI-generated scene that visually connects the 3 good things (landscape, cityscape, or abstract art with 3 colored marker pins)
3. The 3 good things listed below with color-coded dots matching the markers
4. Bloom branding

### Card Specification (MVP)

- **Primary format:** 1080x1920px (9:16 vertical, optimized for Instagram Stories and TikTok)
- **Secondary format:** 1080x1080px (square, for Instagram feed and X)
- **Date header:** Top 120px, #2d2d2d background, white text, Inter or SF Pro font, month + year left-aligned, day number right-aligned
- **Scene area:** Middle ~55% of card, AI-generated image fills this zone
- **3 colored markers:** Frontend-composited via HTML Canvas/SVG overlay on the AI scene (not generated within the AI image itself). Fixed positions: bottom-left, center-top, bottom-right. Colors: pink (#e8a0bf), green (#7bb38a), gold (#e8c77b)
- **Text area:** Bottom ~35%, cream (#fdf6ee) background, Georgia serif for good things text (14px equivalent), color dots matching markers
- **Brand mark:** "BLOOM" in 10px caps, #c4b8a8, bottom-right corner
- **Referral link:** Small "bloom.app" URL at bottom edge, serving as watermark + acquisition channel

### Sharing Constraints

Instagram does not offer a public API for PWAs to post Stories directly. The sharing flow is:
- **X (Twitter):** Web Intent API shares a public page URL (e.g., bloom.app/card/abc123) with Open Graph meta tags. X crawls the OG image for the card preview. Requires each card to have a public permalink with proper OG tags hosted on Supabase Storage
- **Instagram:** "Download to camera roll" + deep link to Instagram (via `instagram://story-camera` on mobile). Requires 2 steps from user
- **General:** Web Share API (navigator.share) on supported browsers for native share sheet
- **Fallback:** Download button always available. Users manually share the downloaded image

### Prompt Engineering Strategy

The scene synthesis step (3 inputs → image prompt) is the core quality bottleneck. GPT-4o mini receives a system prompt that:
1. Extracts the emotional tone and key visual elements from each input
2. Combines them into a single coherent scene description (landscape, interior, or abstract)
3. Places 3 distinct visual anchors in the scene corresponding to the 3 inputs
4. Enforces a consistent art style constraint (e.g., "soft watercolor illustration, warm tones, studio ghibli-inspired")
5. Handles low-quality inputs gracefully (if user writes "good day" with no detail, the system adds generic positive imagery rather than producing a blank scene)

This prompt must be tested against 50+ diverse input combinations before launch. Track "card quality rating" as the primary quality metric.

### Failure Handling

If AI image generation fails (timeout, API error, content filter):
1. Retry once with simplified prompt
2. If retry fails, generate card with a preset gradient background (using the 3 marker colors) instead of AI scene
3. Show "regenerate" button so user can try again later
4. Log failure for monitoring

### MVP Scope Boundary

This document constrains implementation to Phase 1 (MVP) only. Future phases are directional only:
- Phase 2: Calendar view showing month of scenes
- Phase 3: Preset style templates + custom styles
- Phase 4+: 3D interactive scenes, video output (long-term vision, not to influence MVP architecture)

## Card Compositing (Eng Review 决策)

卡片最终 PNG 由 `@vercel/og` (satori) 在服务端合成，**不在浏览器 Canvas 完成**。

**理由：**
- 浏览器 Canvas 加载 Supabase Storage 图片时受 CORS 限制，`toDataURL()` 易失败
- 一个 `/api/card/[id].png` 接口同时承担 X/Twitter OG 图爬取和用户下载，两件事合一
- iOS Safari 对跨域 Canvas 有额外限制，服务端合成绕过所有兼容性问题

**接口设计：**
- `GET /api/card/[entryId].png` → 返回合成后的 PNG
- 首次请求：从 DB 读 entry → 从 Supabase Storage 读场景图 → satori 合成 → 写入 Supabase Storage `cards/[entryId].png` → 返回 PNG
- 二次请求：直接 302 到缓存 URL，避免重复合成消耗 function 时间
- 缓存命中率应 >95%（每张卡片只在生成时合成一次）

**合成内容：** 顶部日期黑条 + 中部场景图 + 三个固定位置图钉 (SVG overlay) + 底部奶白色文字区 + Bloom 品牌标记。所有元素布局在 satori JSX 中声明式定义。

## Engineering Principles (MVP)

1. **API 路由层薄、business logic 在 `lib/`** — `app/api/*/route.ts` 只做参数校验、调度、返回；真正逻辑放 `lib/scene/`、`lib/card/`，方便单测与替换
2. **图片 API 抽象为 provider 接口** — `lib/scene/imageProvider.ts` 定义 `ImageProvider`，实现 `FluxSchnellProvider` / `DalleProvider`，启动时按 env 选择
3. **Prompt 模板单文件、版本化** — `lib/scene/promptTemplate.ts` 导出 `SYSTEM_PROMPT_V1`；DB `entries` 表加 `prompt_version` 字段，便于回归
4. **失败必须有视觉反馈** — 任何 AI 步骤失败需 toast + fallback (灰度渐变) + 重试按钮，禁止静默失败 (会拉低分享率分母)
5. **不写未来代码** — Phase 2/3/4 是方向性的，不影响 MVP 架构。看到 `// TODO: support 3d` 这类代码立即删除

## Input Interaction States (Design Review 决策, 2026-05-04)

通过两轮 craft / behavioral 双 agent 辩论收敛得出。指导原则：**Ghibli 的皮肤，BeReal 的神经末梢，但绝不长 Duolingo 的心脏。**

### 输入字段行为 (D4)

- 字段 1 输入完成后，光标停留 1.2s 柔和失焦——**绝不 auto-focus 字段 2** (gratitude 不是表单)
- 字段 2/3 在前一字段完成后做 0.4s 呼吸式微亮 (border 1px → 2px solid 对应 marker 色)
- 输入时该字段 marker 在底部按钮上方 fade in
- 输入完成且非空时，对应 marker 保持点亮 + 0.15s ✓ check 微动画
- Tab/Enter 键支持手动跳转 (键盘用户 affordance)

### Placeholder 文案 (D6 + D9)

人工撰写 60-90 条池子，按本地日期每日轮换 (同一天打开多次相同)。文案必须**具体到几乎私密的程度**：

示例池：
- "the way oat milk curled in my coffee this morning"
- "a stranger held the door for me"
- "the bus was 4 minutes late but I didn't care"
- "my sister texted me a meme at 2pm"
- "the way morning light hit the kitchen counter"
- "the radiator clicking on" (冬季)

**禁止：** "Grateful for family / health / friends"、"What made you smile today?"、"Three things you're grateful for..." 这类 wellness app 死亡话术。Placeholder 是产品的第一句话——它教用户"我们在乎什么样的细节"。

时段微调：早 9 点前偏早餐/通勤、下午偏午后场景、周日偏慢节奏。**不做** AI 生成、地理定位、说教式金句。

### Generate 按钮状态

- 0/3 填: opacity 30%, 灰色文字 "Generate"
- 1-2/3 填: opacity 60%, 文字变 "Add 2 more..." / "Add 1 more..."
- 3/3 填: opacity 100% + 渐变背景 + 1.5s 周期 soft pulse
- Pressed: scale(0.97) + haptic feedback (mobile)

### Desktop 布局

- 整体最大宽度 1040px，居中，大面积 cream 留白
- 上：3 输入字段单列，最大宽 480px
- 下：9:16 卡片预览以 70% 实际尺寸悬浮，**只是空白 cream 纸 + 三个未着色 marker 占位**——不实时镜像用户输入
- 用户停止输入 2s 后做一次极轻纸纹呼吸 ("我在听" 反馈)
- 生成态：卡片在原位放大到 100% 执行 Reveal Ritual

**核心判断：** Bloom 是手机产品，桌面是它的"安静版本"不是"加强版"。预览框是"等待被填满的纸"，不是镜子。**禁止** Figma 式左右分栏、实时预览、卡片占满屏。

### Accessibility — 形状 + 颜色双编码

| Marker | 颜色 | 形状 | Hint |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Pink #e8a0bf | 圆点 | 心形 |
| 2 | Green #7bb38a | 方块 | 叶形 |
| 3 | Gold #e8c77b | 菱形 | 星形 |

Screen reader: "First gratitude, marked with pink heart..."

**双重收益：** 8% 男性色盲用户能识别 + Instagram 缩略图 (<200px) 内仍可辨识 = 直接服务 sharing rate ≥15% KPI。Accessibility 和增长是同一件事。

## Generation & Reveal Ritual

### 生成等待 (5-15s) — 死亡区设计 (D5)

**核心判断：** 焦虑来自"是否在进行"而非"等多久"。持续的水彩动态本身就是 visible work (Labor illusion 真实满足)，不需要剧场化的剥皮过程。

时间线：
- **0-4s：** 用户的 3 句话以手写体**整体淡入** (非逐字打字机) 到 cream 纸面
- **4-12s：** 水彩从文字下方向外缓慢晕染，颜色由浅至深，边缘保持柔和未定型
- Loop 第二次完成时，中心微微凝聚为光点 (暗示接近完成)
- **兜底：** 实际 >12s 时，第 10s 出现一行手写体小字 "正在晕开…"，仅此一次
- 极轻环境音可选，默认开启 -24dB (设置可关)

**绝对禁止：** progress bar、百分比、spinner、"AI is creating..." 文案、用户文字的打字机动画、骨架屏 (skeleton screen)、任何"我在用 AI 工具"的认知触发。

### 卡片揭晓 — 2.4s Ghibli 水彩 reveal (D7)

时间线：
1. Cream 背景已在 (从等待状态延续)
2. 水彩底色从中心晕开 (~800ms)
3. 三色点 (墨滴入水) 依次落下，每点 150ms，60ms stagger，每个 0.05s scale 1.1→1.0 微弹
4. 手写体文字最后淡入，每行错开 200ms (~600ms)
5. 完成后 400ms：卡片整体下沉 2px + 极浅投影 ("纸张沉降" 完形结尾)
6. 800ms 停留让用户消化
7. share/save/regenerate 按钮 fade in (300ms)

多感官层：
- 第一个色点落下时触发**一次**极轻 haptic (`UIImpactFeedbackGenerator .soft` on iOS)
- 可选封蜡音效 (默认**关闭**)，开启时是一声极短纸张轻响——**不是**盖章音

**绝对禁止：** 信封从底部"寄到"动画、连续盖章音效、"Your card is ready ✨" copy、烟花/粒子特效、滑入。任何让用户说"哇好酷"的动画都失败——目标是用户屏住呼吸。

## Retention Philosophy (D8) — 反 Duolingo 立场

**双 agent 共识，最重要的产品定义决策：**

> Bloom 的增长引擎就是它的美感本身。任何稀释美感的行为机制都是锯自己坐的树枝——分享率 15% 比 7 天留存 40% 更脆弱，而前者只能靠 craft 赢得。

| 机制 | 决定 | 理由 |
|---|---|---|
| Streak 数字 | **完全删除**——首页不显示，分享卡片也不显示 | streak 焦虑会赶走非核心用户，且稀释美感 |
| Day 2 邮件 | **opt-in，默认关闭**。首张卡分享页底部一次性轻问 "Want a gentle morning nudge tomorrow?" (yes/no/never) | 用户主动选择 = 尊重 |
| 限定色彩 | **保留但完全隐形**：连续 7 天后 "morning fog blue" 自动可用，**无 toast、无徽章、无"恭喜解锁"提示** | 用户下次调色时自然发现 = delight 而非 mechanic |
| Push notifications | 不做 (PWA iOS 不可靠且违反此哲学) | - |
| 红点徽章 | **绝对禁止** | 道德底线 |
| "你已 X 天没记录" 文案 | **绝对禁止** | 道德底线 |
| 朋友们都在记录的 social proof | **绝对禁止** | 道德底线 |
